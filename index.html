Solve in Three

Welcome to Solve in Three

The goal of this puzzle

is to arrange these six coins...

...into the shape of a ring.

There are two rules:

A coin can only move

where it will touch two or more coins.

And, a coin cannot move

if it's trapped by the surrounding coins.

This puzzle can be solved in many ways.

But there's only one way to solve it in three moves.
